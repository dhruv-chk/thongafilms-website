About Us

"Thonga" is a Bengali word for a paper bag. These are one of the cheapest available bags to carry small goods and items. It is mostly handmade and is made of daily newspapers and tabloids.

If you still did not get the shape, size and ease of making it. Watch this DIY video.

So, what makes our work so "Thonga"?

It is that simplicity, ease, honesty and rawness which defines our films... not to forget we deliver videos at the best available rates for our clients.

Since its inception till now, Thonga Films have sbeen involved in many projects. Some of the notable ones are listed below:

  1. Uniworld Premiere League Season 2 Video partners
  2. Produced Bombay Mix (Chanachur) which received Official Selection at Calcutta International Cult Film Festival. The makers are the core members of our team.
  3. Video Content consultants for McNally Sayaji Engineering Ltd.
