Maithon - What Could Have Been

Maithon is a small town on the borders of Jharkhand and West Bengal. The place is historically important as Nehru flagged off the Damodar Valley Corporation project at Maithon.
Sadly the place is now subject to uncontrolled tourism. Industries have come up in and around. This video is an effort to show what could have been if the pollution, tourism and industrialization were kept in check. The footage have been heavily color corrected and graded; the color of the polluted water has been changed dramatically to catch the eye; polluted sky has been replaced by clear, bright sky.

Hum Paani Hain

An indie (independent) filmmaker, tired of his corporate film making gigs and superficial client appeasing advertisements, comes across the " WE ART WATER 3 " film festival. He sees in it a golden opportunity to invest his time and creativity into a non-commercial film, albeit short but the content of which is relevant in the current context of alarming scarcity of a basic necessity like water. He calls up a junior of his and tells him about the competition and tries to get him interested by adding that if they win a few of more such awards, the combined cash prize will enable them to finally make a proper feature film, independently. However, his primary reason to make the film is to highlight and talk about the higher cause through the film. Or, is it ? Does he actually care ? Do WE really care ?...
